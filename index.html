Hi, I'm Lamia - a design and creative enthusiast seeking to build new skills every opportunity I get.

I was born in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia and moved to the United States 21 days post birth. Raised in Colorado, I had a lot to learn about different cultures everyday. I had grown to develop multiple perspectives and the different ways to communicate with different people in order to meet their expectations of satisfaction and happiness. Happiness is something I cherish (aside from innovation of course) and I love to be the person to bring that into other's lives.

I found a love for tech and design during my high school years at Legend High. My freshman year of High School I took introductory engineering courses and later proceeded to take courses in Graphic Design and the Adobe Creative Suite. I realized that although the two can be seen as different concepts, one having focus on colorful aesthetics and the other being more technical they both shared the value of design. To build something out of nothing and having a purpose. This realization built a desire within me to have this balance between practical design and visual design.

I'm an undergrad at the University of Colorado, Denver, pursuing a B.A. in Computer Science with plans to minor in Digital Design. Spring 2020, I took a Human-Centered Design course which raised my interest in the Product Design industry. Simultaneously, I was taking a Web API course learning web development from front-end to back-end which further grew my interest in design.

Outside of design, I also love film editing, and spend a lot of my free time editing footage. I also really enjoy learning new technical skills without any limitations (animation, creating projects, etc) as well as day to day skills such as Baking or Cooking. Some fun facts about me are that I'm a sweet tooth but also love everything and anything that's spicy (Sirarcha might as well be a seasoning).

I'm always enthusiastic about gaining new experiences. Reach out and let's chat!
